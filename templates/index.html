What our project is about?

(briefly speaking :)

Our project is about splitting the secret among, say, four teaching assistants (TAs), using a threshold secret-sharing scheme, so that any three TAs could jointly recover the secret, but any two TAs could not. Also our algorithm use GF(2). It means that we have 10 six-dimensional vector type GF (2), which must be composed of 0 and 1
